Group project

An affordable home automation system that is capable of controlling electronics such as lights, air conditioning, blinds, audio visual equipment, security systems and irrigation systems specifically but not exclusively for people with disabilities. The system can be controlled via eye motion, gestures, and voice as well as conventional methods such as a tactile remote and phone or tablet to cater for many forms of impairment or abilities. Today, systems such as this are available but can be very expensive and require an enormous amount of work from specialist trades therefore are unattainable for many people in the community. Our project will be a cloud-based software as a service solution alongside a priority networked processor with inputs and outputs such as infrared, RS232 and dry contact relays and physical remote.
